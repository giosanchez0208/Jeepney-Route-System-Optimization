How is this an "Hybrid GA with ACO-inspired Local Search"
if the method is so unconventional?

Three pillars met.
1. Path-cost deposition: Pheromones are deposited inversely proportional to path cost ($Q / C(\pi_p)$).
2. Generational evaporation: Pheromones decay over time ($\rho$) to prevent search stagnation.
3. Probabilistic biasing: The algorithm uses the pheromone matrix to weight its decisions (in your case, crossover and mutation probabilities) rather than making purely random choices.
